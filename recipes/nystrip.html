Chicken Parm

Juicy NY Strip with loads of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2x 1 pound NY Strip boneless steaks
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ground pepper
  • 2 pads of organic butter

Steps

  1. Melt butter over low heat in a small bowl
  2. Coat one side of each steak with butter
  3. Apply a liberal coating of sea salt and ground pepper to the buttered side of the steak
  4. Flip each steak over and repeat steaps 1 -3 for the other side
  5. Cook on grill at 500* for 8 minutes
  6. Flip steaks over, and cook for another 8 minutes, checking internal temperature until they reach 145*
