Chicken Parm

Zesty baked cajun salmon!

Ingredients

  • 2 pound Atlantic Salmon filet
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp Zatarians Cajun Seasoning
  • tin foil

Steps

  1. Spead tin foil across a cookie sheet, creating a "bowl"
  2. Lay salmon filet across the tin foil
  3. Appli a liberla coating to taste of the Zatarians Cajun seasoning
  4. Place cookie sheet in preheated 400* oven for 28 minutes
  5. Transfer salmon in 8 to 10oz slices to paltes and serve
