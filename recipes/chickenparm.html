Chicken Parm

Tasty home made chicken parm!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a shallow bowl and set aside.
  2. Mix bread crumbs in a separate bowl, set aside.
  3. Dip chicken breast in beaten eggs. Transfer breast to the bread crumb mixture, pressing crumbs into both sides. Repeat for each breast. Let chicken rest for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Cook chicken in the hot olive oil until golden, about 2 minutes per side. The chicken will finish cooking in the oven.
  5. Transfer chicken to a baking dish, and top each breast with 2 tablespoons tomato sauce.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until chicken breasts are no longer pink in the center, 15 to 20 minutes.
